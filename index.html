Virat Kohli

Indian cricketer
Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the India national team.
A right-handed top-order batsman, Kohli is regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world.
He plays for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League, and has been the team's captain since 2013. Wikipidea
